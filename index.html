Craft Beer Analysis ETL

An ETL Project (extract, transform, load) where our team manipulated a dataset on craft beers in the United States to analyze which beers were most popular, where they were being brewed, and which states produced the most craft beer. Very surface level market research that could be used to determine where to build a brewery, or what type of beer to brew in certain locations based on principles of supply and demand. My role was in the web design, and analytics. The site features a responsive navbar and drop down, made with Javascript. Everyone had a hand in trasforming our dataset with tools such as PANDAS to create multiple different dataframes in which we could graph from.
